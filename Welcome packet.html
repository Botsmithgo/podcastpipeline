How the Process Works

Every episode follows a proven sequence designed to maximize both production quality and relationship depth.

01
Strategy Setup

We finalize your ICP, guest priorities, and overall show positioning. This becomes the foundation for every decision that follows.

02
Outreach Launch

We begin inviting ideal guests using personalized, thoughtful outreach. No spam—only genuine invitations that respect their time.

03
